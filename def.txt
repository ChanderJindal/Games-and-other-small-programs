<section data-src="hm">
<h2>smoke</h2> <span class="snd" data-snd="S0498500">
</span> <span class="pron" onclick="pron_key()">(sm&#x14d;k)</span>
<div class="pseg">
<i>n.</i>
<div class="ds-list">
<b>1. </b>
<div class="sds-list">
<b>a. </b> A mixture of gases and small suspended particles of soot or other solids, resulting from the burning of materials such as wood or coal.</div>
<div class="sds-list">
<b>b. </b> A cloud of such gases and suspended particles.</div>
<div class="sds-list">
<b>c. </b> A vapor, mist, or fume that resembles this.</div>
</div>
<div class="ds-list">
<b>2. </b> Something insubstantial, unreal, or transitory: <span class="illustration">"What everybody echoes or in silence passes by as true to-day may turn out to be falsehood to-morrow, mere smoke of opinion"</span> <span class="illustration">(Henry David Thoreau).</span>
</div>
<div class="ds-list">
<b>3. </b>
<div class="sds-list">
<b>a. </b> The act of smoking a form of tobacco: <span class="illustration">went out for a smoke.</span>
</div>
<div class="sds-list">
<b>b. </b> The duration of this act.</div>
</div>
<div class="ds-list">
<b>4. </b> <i>Informal</i> Tobacco in a form that can be smoked, especially a cigarette: <span class="illustration">money to buy smokes.</span>
</div>
<div class="ds-list">
<b>5. </b> A substance used in warfare to produce a smokescreen.</div>
<div class="ds-list">
<b>6. </b> Something used to conceal or obscure.</div>
<div class="ds-list">
<b>7. </b> A pale to grayish blue to bluish or dark gray.</div>
<div class="ds-list">
<b>8. </b> <i>Baseball</i> Pitches thrown at high velocity; fast balls: <span class="illustration">threw a lot of smoke in the early innings.</span>
</div>
</div>
<div class="pseg">
<i>v.</i> <b>smoked</b>, <b>smok·ing</b>, <b>smokes</b> </div>
<div class="pseg">
<i>v.</i>
<i>intr.</i>
<div class="ds-list">
<b>1. </b>
<div class="sds-list">
<b>a. </b> To draw in and exhale smoke from a cigarette, cigar, or pipe: <span class="illustration">It's forbidden to smoke here.</span>
</div>
<div class="sds-list">
<b>b. </b> To engage in smoking regularly or habitually: <span class="illustration">He smoked for years before stopping.</span>
</div>
</div>
<div class="ds-list">
<b>2. </b> To emit smoke or a smokelike substance: <span class="illustration">chimneys smoking in the cold air.</span>
</div>
<div class="ds-list">
<b>3. </b> To emit smoke excessively: <span class="illustration">The station wagon smoked even after the tune-up.</span>
</div>
<div class="ds-list">
<b>4. </b> <i>Slang</i> <div class="sds-list">
<b>a. </b> To go or proceed at high speed.</div>
<div class="sds-list">
<b>b. </b> To play or perform energetically: <span class="illustration">The band was really smoking in the second set.</span>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="pseg">
<i>v.</i>
<i>tr.</i>
<div class="ds-list">
<b>1. </b>
<div class="sds-list">
<b>a. </b> To draw in and exhale the smoke of (tobacco, for example): <span class="illustration">I've never smoked a panatela.</span>
</div>
<div class="sds-list">
<b>b. </b> To do so regularly or habitually: <span class="illustration">I used to smoke filtered cigarettes.</span>
